TEST.BAS
A program to test 6809-strlen
by Richard John Foster Cavell (c) 2017, 2018, 2019, 2025
https://github.com/richardcavell/6809-strlen

This program is for an emulated TRS-80 Color Computer 2 with Disk Extended
Color BASIC, running in XRoar or MAME.  It needs at least 32K of RAM, and a
disk drive.

1. You can change the default language by editing line 1000.

2. You can simulate errors in the assembly language routines by editing
   line 1100.

Richard Cavell
